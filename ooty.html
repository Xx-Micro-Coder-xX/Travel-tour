~Ooty~

Welcome to Ooty! Its time to explore the wonder of nature with also some breath taking hill sights to see and explore. And also get ready to learn about some mouth watering food and snacks that you might prefer to have if you are planning to go there as a vacation.

Sight Seeing Places

Over here I have shown some of the most beautiful scenry places in ooty to see and explore. As you explore you will learn about the places you will visit.

Ooty Lake

One of the most important parts of every Ooty tour, Ooty lake is indeed a place to visit.There is boating house located near the lake offering a wide array of boats on hire. Cycling by the side of lake is also worth experiencing. There are also a few shops located around the lake, selling various locally made items.

Kalhatty Waterfalls

Kalhatty Waterfalls is one of the most beautiful waterfalls that you will see on an Ooty Tour. This waterfall can be reached via a trek of 2 mile from Kalahatti village. Over here if you brought your binoculars you can also bird watch, as lots of birds live here. Its scenic beauty is sure to leave you spellbound.

Ooty Rose Garden

Rose Garden is another popular place to visit in Ooty. Maintained by Government of Tamil Nadu, this garden is spread across 4 hectares of land and proudly presents more than 20 thousand variety of roses. The unparalleled beauty of roses promises a visual treat and an ideal backdrop for photography.

Deer Park

Located at a distance of 2 km from Ooty Lake, Deer Park is easily accessible by road. It is one of the known wildlife sanctuaries of not only Tamil Nadu but of India presenting amazing opportunity to observe the wildlife and various animals from closer.
