Welcome to Hampi! Its time to explore the ancient history with many types of temples that was made by kings thousands of years ago. And also get ready to learn about some mouth watering food and snacks that you might prefer to have if you are planning to go there as a vacation

Sight Seeing Places

Over here I have shown some of the ancient temples and sculptures in Hampi to see and explore. As you explore you will learn about the places you will visit.

Virupaksha Temple

The temple is so constructed that its main entrance faces east. There is a pillared hall in the center which is reckoned as the Ranga Mandapa.The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva, known here as Virupaksha. It has an elephant stable and be carful as there are many monkeys in the temple.

Lotus Mahal

The Lotus Mahal is a two storeyed pleasure temple situated in the zone of the royal performance. It is built in a mixed style with Hindu and Muslim architectural features. It is constructed by the rescembling the water Lotus.

Hazara Rama Temple

This beautiful temple marks its presence at the central focus of a royal area and is dedicated to Lord Rama.Towards its northern side, you may find a lawn widely stretched apart. The temple has two huge getaways and the interior homes to sculpted columns.

Sanapur Lake

One of the best places to visit in Hampi is Sanapur Lake. Located around 5 kilometres away from Hippie Island, this lake is surrounded by huge rocks and boulders.One of the major activities you can do here is cliff jumping or taking a ride in a Coracle. Coracles are circular boats which are native to this region. Also known as a country boat here, it can carry around six to eight people at a time. It is an activity you should enjoy at least once in your lifetime.
